Key non-negotiables for Après Beach:
- The two-color rule. Navy (#1B2C4F, PMS 289 C) and sand (#F1E9D7, unbleached linen) do every job. Never #000, never #FFF.
- Seasonal accents (coral, turquoise, sage) are guests — styling, fabric, editorial only. Never on wordmark, primary CTA, or system surfaces.
- Display face is Gatsby Prelude Sans (in fonts/), all-caps with proper accents. Body falls back to Söhne → Inter.
- Photography is the brand. Heritage golden-hour editorial only. No B&W, no flash, no noon.
- Voice: considered, low-volume, slightly knowing. No exclamation, no emoji, sentence case in body and buttons.
- Motion: linen falls, doesn't snap. cubic-bezier(0.22, 0.61, 0.36, 1), 240ms default. No bounce.
- Hover/active/disabled = tonal shifts within the same color. Never new hues.
